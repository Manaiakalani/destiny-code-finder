# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Destiny 2 players ("Guardians") who want a fast, trustworthy way to discover, verify, copy, and redeem promotional codes. They may be checking a newly shared code on desktop or redeeming several codes quickly from a phone.

## Product Purpose

Code Vault consolidates verified Destiny redemption codes and their reward imagery into a single searchable archive. Success means users can immediately understand which codes are usable, find the reward they want, and move from discovery to Bungie's redemption flow with confidence and minimal effort.

## Brand Personality

Focused, mysterious, precise. The product should feel authentically inspired by Destiny's restrained sci-fi interface language while remaining clearly independent, community-built, and task-first.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a noisy gamer fan site with constant glow, motion, competing subclass colors, novelty effects, or ornamental UI that obscures the task. Avoid generic SaaS dashboard chrome and avoid copying Bungie's official interface so closely that affiliation could be implied.

## Design Principles

1. Put redemption first: prioritize finding, copying, and redeeming a code over decorative storytelling.
2. Earn trust visibly: make verification status, source, availability, and update freshness easy to understand.
3. Use Destiny flavor with restraint: atmosphere should frame the task, not compete with it.
4. Support scanning before reading: organize codes and metadata for rapid comparison and recognition.
5. Make every state actionable: loading, empty, error, copied, unavailable, and user-added states should explain what happened and what to do next.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Preserve keyboard-only operation, visible focus states, semantic status announcements, 44px minimum touch targets, text alternatives for reward imagery, and layouts that remain usable at 200% zoom. Never rely on subclass color alone for meaning. Respect reduced-motion preferences and ensure all three themes maintain readable contrast.
